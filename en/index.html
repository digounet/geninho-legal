Frequently asked questions

Your questions, answered.

Does the app require parent approval to mark study as complete?

For everyday study and exam prep, completion is based on quiz performance. Parent approval still happens for rewards.

Can I import several pages of a book at once?

Yes. Send multiple pages and the app organizes the topics automatically.

Does YouTube show up for every student?

No. It only appears when you turn the permission on for that child.

Does the quiz use on-device AI?

No. The quiz uses online AI, and every question is reviewed before the student sees it.

Can I check things without opening the app?

Yes. Use Siri shortcuts to see today's classes and exam topics by subject.

Can I complete a study session on Apple Watch?

The Watch shows today's sessions and sends the request; completion happens securely on the iPhone.

Does Geninho's free-form chat answer anything?

No. The chat stays focused on school topics and never gives the answer away. Off-topic or sensitive questions are flagged for parents to review.

Is my data safe?

We use Sign in with Apple, Google, and Face ID / Touch ID. Schedule photos are processed on the iPhone itself — the images never leave your device. Full details in the Privacy Policy.
