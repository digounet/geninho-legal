- **OpenAI** (ChatGPT / GPT),

- factually, logically, or mathematically incorrect;
- out of date with the current school curriculum;

- occasionally sensitive, offensive, or out of scope, despite the moderation mechanisms.

### 6.2 We do not author or edit the generated content
Geninho **does not manually write** the questions, answers, or explanations produced by the AI. We act as a **technological intermediary**: we send the required context to the AI provider and display the response to the user inside a controlled experience. For that reason, **we are not responsible for the specific content** generated by the AI on each interaction.

### 6.3 Mitigation mechanisms we offer
To reduce risks, Geninho provides:
- **automated review** of the quiz before it is shown to the student;
- **shuffled** answer options;
- a **"replace question"** button that logs feedback;
- **automated classification** of free-chat questions, blocking clearly inappropriate content;
- a **guardian review panel** for flagged questions (direct answer keys, sensitive content, suspicious requests);
- a standing recommendation to **verify** OCR-imported data manually.

These mechanisms are a risk-reduction effort, **not a guarantee that content is 100% correct or appropriate**.
